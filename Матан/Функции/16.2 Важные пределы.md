### $\left( 1 + \frac{1}{x} \right)^{x}\to e$ при $x\to \infty$

1. $x\to+ \infty$ при $x>1$
   $0<[x]\leq x <[x]+1$
   $\dfrac{1}{[x]}\geq \dfrac{1}{x}> \dfrac{1}{[x]+ 1}$
   $1 + \dfrac{1}{[x]+ 1}<1+ \frac{1}{x}\leq 1 + \frac{1}{[x]}$
   $\left( 1+\frac{1}{[x]+1} \right)^{[x]}<\left( 1+\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x}<\left( 1+\frac{1}{[x]} \right)^{[x]+1}$

	$\forall x>n_{\varepsilon}:e-\varepsilon<\left( 1+\frac{1}{[x]+1} \right)^{[x]}<\left( 1 +\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x}<\left( 1+\frac{1}{[x]} \right)^{[x]+1}<e+\varepsilon$
	$\implies\left( 1+\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x}\to e$ при $x\to +\infty$

2. $x\to-\infty$ $t:=-x\to+\infty$
   $\left( 1+\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x}=\left( 1-\frac{1}{t} \right)^{-t}=\left( \frac{t}{t-1} \right)^{t}=\left( 1+\frac{1}{t-1} \right)^{t}=\left( 1+\frac{1}{t-1} \right)^{t-1}\cdot \left( 1 + \frac{1}{t-1} \right)\to e$
   
   $\forall\varepsilon>0\exists\delta_{\varepsilon}^{+}>0,\forall x>\delta_{\varepsilon}^{+}$
	$\left|\left( 1 +\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x}-e \right|<\varepsilon$
	
   $\forall\delta_{\varepsilon}>0,\forall x<-\delta_{\varepsilon}^{-}$
	$\left|\left( 1 +\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x}-e \right|<\varepsilon$
	
	$\forall|x|>\max\{ \delta_{\varepsilon}^{+};\delta_{\varepsilon}^{-} \}=:\delta_{\varepsilon}$
	$\left| \left( 1+\frac{1}{x}-e \right) \right|<\varepsilon$
