$\vec{r}_{A}=\vec{r}_{AO}+\vec{r_{O}}$
$\vec{r_{c}}=\frac{{\int \vec{r_{0}}dm}}{m}$
$I_{A}=\int \vec{r}_{A}^{2}dm=\int(\vec{r}_{AO}+\vec{r}_{O})^{2}dm=\cancelto{ \ mr_{AO}^{2} }{ \int r_{AO}^{2}dm }+\cancelto{ \ I_{0} }{ \int r_{0}^{2}dm }+\cancelto{ \ 0 }{ 2\int(\vec{r}_{AO},\vec{r_{O}})dm }$
Если $O$ — центр масс, то: $\vec{r_{c}}=0\implies2\int(\vec{r}_{AO},\vec{r_{O}})dm=0$

$\boxed{I_{A}=I_{c}+mr_{AO}^{2}}$
